Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-09

Who we are

LiveIT is a real-life RPG app where you complete real-world quests to earn experience, comfort and skill points. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

Account information you provide: email address, username and an optional avatar image. Content you create: quest completions, the photos or videos you upload as proof, captions, comments and reactions. Basic usage data needed to run the service, such as level, XP, comfort score, streaks and the quests you accept.

How we use your information

We use your information to create and maintain your account, show your progress, display the social feed and leaderboards, deliver daily quests, keep the community safe, and improve the app. We do not sell your personal data.

Photos, content and visibility

When you complete a quest and post proof, the photo or video, the quest title and description, your username and your reaction counts are visible to other users in the feed, on your profile and in leaderboards. Do not upload anything you are not comfortable sharing publicly.

Service providers

We host data and authentication with Supabase. If you choose social sign-in, Google or Apple process your sign-in. These providers handle your data under their own terms and only to provide their service to us.

Data retention

We keep your information while your account is active. When you delete your account, your profile, quest history and uploaded content are removed from the app, except where we must retain limited records to comply with the law or resolve disputes.

Your rights and choices

You can edit your profile, change your language, block other users, report content, and permanently delete your account at any time from Settings. Depending on where you live, you may also have rights to access or correct your data — contact us to exercise them.

Safety and moderation

You can report content and block users directly in the app. We review reports and may remove content or suspend accounts that violate our Terms. We act on objectionable-content reports promptly.

Children

LiveIT is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Keep your password private.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the date above and, for material changes, notify you in the app.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at benchiitrade@gmail.com.
